Range of Cars

Coupes

Coupes are four-wheeled enclosed carriage for two passengers and a driver.

SUVs

An SUV is sport utility vehicle is a vehicle classified as a light truck, but operated as a family vehicle.

Convertibles

A convertible is an automobile body style that can convert between an open-air mode and an enclosed one, varying in degree and means by model.

Sedans

Sedans are enclosed automobile for four or more people, having two or four doors.
